About Me

Hobbies of mine include biking, backpacking, camping, and traveling. I have moved around a lot and my hobbies have adapted with me. Minnesota has awesome biking, cross country skiing, and canoeing! I hope this summer to backpack part of the Superior Hiking Trail!
